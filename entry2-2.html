Hereditary (2018)


  • Director: Ari Aster
  • Cast: Toni Collette, Alex Wolff,
    Milly Shapiro, Gabriel Byrne

After the death of her secretive mother, Annie Graham (Toni Collette) begins to uncover disturbing truths about her family’s history. As she tries to cope with her grief and unravel the mystery behind her mother's past, a series of increasingly horrific events unfold, revealing dark and supernatural elements tied to her family.

"Hereditary" was a deeply unsettling look at family trauma and grief. Toni Collette's performance was haunting and made me feel her despair. The buildup of tension led to a shocking ending that really got under my skin.

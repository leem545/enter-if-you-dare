slasher

A slasher horror movie is a subgenre of horror that typically involves a killer who stalks and murders a group of people, often in violent and graphic ways. The killer usually uses sharp, bladed weapons, which is where the term "slasher" comes from.

American Psycho (2000)


  • Director: : Mary Harron
  • Cast: Christian Bale, Willem Dafoe,
    Jared Leto, Chloë Sevigny, Reese
    Witherspoon, Samantha Mathis,
    Justin Theroux, Josh Lucas

Set in the 1980s, the film follows Patrick Bateman (Christian Bale), a wealthy New York City investment banker with a dark, psychopathic alter ego. As his bloodlust grows, he begins committing increasingly violent murders while maintaining his facade in the high-powered world of finance.

I found "American Psycho" to be a darkly funny take on consumer culture. Christian Bale's portrayal of Patrick Bateman was both charming and creepy, making it hard to look away. The shocking violence and social critique really stuck with me.

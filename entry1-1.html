You’re next (2011)


  • Director: Adam Wingard
  • Cast: Sharni Vinson, Nicholas Tucci,
    Wendy Glenn, AJ Bowen, Joe Swanberg,
    Amy Seimetz, Barbara Crampton, Rob Moran

A family reunion at a secluded country house takes a terrifying turn when a group of masked intruders begins a brutal attack on the family. However, the attackers quickly find themselves in a deadly fight when one of the guests, Erin (Sharni Vinson), proves to be more capable than they anticipated, using her survivalist skills to turn the tables.

I enjoyed "You’re Next" for its fresh twist on the home invasion story. The surprises kept me on my toes, making it more than just a typical slasher.

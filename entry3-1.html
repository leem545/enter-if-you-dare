Insidious (2010)


  • Director: James Wan
  • Cast: Patrick Wilson, Rose Byrne,
    Ty Simpkins, Lin Shaye

When a young boy falls into a mysterious coma, his family begins to experience unsettling paranormal events. Desperate for answers, they enlist the help of a paranormal expert, who reveals that their son is trapped in a dark, spirit-infested dimension called "The Further," and a malevolent entity is trying to possess him.

"Insidious" combined creepy haunted house vibes with an interesting twist on astral projection. It built up a tense atmosphere and had some great jump scares. Patrick Wilson’s performance added to the eerie feeling, making it memorable.

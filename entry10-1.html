I Am Legend (2007)


  • Director: Francis Lawrence
  • Cast: Will Smith, Alice Braga,
    Charlie Tahan

After a virus intended to cure cancer mutates and wipes out most of humanity, turning survivors into light-sensitive, aggressive creatures, Dr. Robert Neville, the last known human in New York City, struggles to find a cure while battling isolation and the infected.

"I Am Legend" is gripping for its depiction of loneliness and survival in a desolate urban landscape, with Will Smith delivering a powerful performance. The film’s suspenseful encounters with mutated creatures and emotional storyline give it a unique blend of action, horror, and drama that makes it memorable within the post-apocalyptic genre.

Sinister (2012)


  • Director: Scott Derrickson
  • Cast: Ethan Hawke, Juliet Rylance,
    Fred Thompson

A true-crime writer discovers a box of home movies in his new house, each depicting horrific murders linked to an ancient demonic entity named Bughuul. As he delves deeper, his family becomes targeted by the supernatural force.

"Sinister" has an atmosphere that creeps up on you, with terrifying use of found footage that feels authentic and haunting. The story unfolds with a disturbing sense of inevitability, and Ethan Hawke brings a grounded vulnerability that makes the supernatural threat feel tangible. The mix of true crime and occult horror is particularly effective, making it a memorable film in the genre.

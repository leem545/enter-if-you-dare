The Host (2006)


  • Director: Bong Joon-ho
  • Cast: Song Kang-ho, Byun Hee-bong,
    Park Hae-il, Bae Doona, Go Ah-sung

After toxic chemicals are dumped into Seoul’s Han River, a monstrous creature emerges, terrorizing the city. The story follows a dysfunctional family that bands together to save their youngest member, who is kidnapped by the creature.

“The Host” is a thrilling blend of monster horror and social commentary. I remember watching this film in a theater in Korea when I was very young, alongside my parents, and it left a deep impression on me. The movie became a huge sensation at the time, and what stood out the most was the incredible acting performances. The creature effects were terrifying, but the portrayal of a family fighting against both a monster and bureaucracy made it even more powerful.

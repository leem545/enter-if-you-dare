Zombie

A zombie movie typically centers on an outbreak where the dead rise as flesh-eating creatures, spreading terror and chaos. These films explore themes of survival, fear of the unknown, and humanity's struggle to retain control in a world overrun by the undead.

28 Days Later (2002)


  • Director: Danny Boyle
  • Cast: Cillian Murphy, Naomie Harris,
    Christopher Eccleston

After a virus outbreak causes extreme rage in those infected, a man wakes up from a coma to find London deserted. He joins a group of survivors, facing the terrifying infected and desperate humans in their quest for safety, forcing them to confront what it truly means to be human in the face of monstrous threats.

"28 Days Later" is intense and atmospheric, with stunning shots of an empty London that capture a true sense of isolation. The rage-infected are terrifying, giving the movie a frantic pace that’s hard to shake off. It’s a gripping journey into both fear and survival, making you wonder what humanity can withstand.

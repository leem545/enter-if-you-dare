Found footage

Found footage horror is a genre where the story is presented through "discovered" video recordings, often filmed by the characters themselves, creating a raw and immersive experience. This style adds a sense of realism and tension, making viewers feel as if they're witnessing terrifying events firsthand.

Paranormal Activity (2007)


  • Director: Oren Peli
  • Cast: Katie Featherston, Micah Sloat

A young couple, Katie and Micah, begin to experience strange occurrences in their home. Suspecting a supernatural presence, they set up a camera to capture evidence, only to realize that the disturbances are far more sinister and relentless than they imagined.

"Paranormal Activity" is terrifying in its simplicity, using everyday home settings to heighten its scares. The slow build-up and subtle noises make the haunting feel disturbingly close to home. It’s a masterclass in creating fear through suspense, and its realistic style will keep you on edge even after the movie ends.

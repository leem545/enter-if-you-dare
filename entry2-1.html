The Others (2001)


  • Director: Alejandro Amenábar
  • Cast: Nicole Kidman, Fionnula Flanagan,
    Christopher Eccleston, Alakina Mann

Grace Stewart (Nicole Kidman) lives in a remote mansion with her two light-sensitive children. When a new group of servants arrives, strange and unsettling events begin to occur. Grace’s increasingly paranoid state and the mansion’s eerie atmosphere lead to a shocking revelation about the true nature of the hauntings.

I loved "The Others" for its creepy atmosphere and strong storyline about loss. Nicole Kidman was fantastic, bringing depth to her mysterious character. The twist at the end completely changed how I viewed the story.

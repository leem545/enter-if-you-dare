Supernatural Horror

Supernatural horror revolves around elements beyond the natural world, often featuring ghosts, demons, witches, or otherworldly forces that evoke fear and unease.

The Conjuring (2013)


  • Director: James Wan
  • Cast: Vera Farmiga, Patrick Wilson,
    Lili Taylor, Ron Livingston

Based on a true story, paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren are called to help a family terrorized by a dark presence in their farmhouse. As the terrifying events escalate, the Warrens confront a malevolent force unlike anything they've encountered before.

I found "The Conjuring" to be a great mix of real scares and heartfelt moments. The characters, Ed and Lorraine Warren, felt authentic and relatable. The way it built suspense while addressing emotional struggles was really effective.

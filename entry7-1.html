Host (2020)


  • Director: Rob Savage
  • Cast: Haley Bishop, Jemma Moore,
    Emma Louise Webb

During the COVID-19 lockdown, a group of friends holds a virtual séance over Zoom for fun. However, things quickly spiral out of control when they unintentionally invite a malicious spirit into their homes. The entire film is shot on the Zoom platform, capturing the characters' reactions as they face supernatural horrors while stuck in isolation.

"Host" brilliantly uses the found footage format in the context of the pandemic, making it relatable and extra terrifying. The unique Zoom setting intensifies the scares, making you feel like you’re part of the séance. Its short runtime packs a punch, delivering relentless suspense that keeps viewers on edge till the last second.

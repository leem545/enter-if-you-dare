Train to Busan (2016)


  • Director: Yeon Sang-ho
  • Cast: Gong Yoo, Ma Dong-seok,
    Jung Yu-mi

During a viral outbreak in South Korea, a group of passengers on a train from Seoul to Busan finds themselves trapped with fast-moving zombies. As they try to survive the journey, they face emotional and physical tests, bringing out both heroism and selfishness among the passengers.

"Train to Busan" is a thrilling, heart-pounding ride that brilliantly mixes action with deep emotional moments. The characters are well-developed, making each loss feel heartbreaking. It’s rare for a zombie movie to evoke so much empathy, and it really redefines the genre with its fresh, high-stakes approach.

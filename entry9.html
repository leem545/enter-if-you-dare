Occult horror

Occult horror is a subgenre that explores supernatural forces, rituals, and dark powers, often centered on themes like witchcraft, demons, and secret societies. This genre taps into fears of the unknown and forbidden, creating tension through the portrayal of mysterious, otherworldly forces that defy human control.

Midsommar (2019)


  • Director: Ari Aster
  • Cast: Florence Pugh, Jack Reynor,
    William Jackson Harper

A group of friends travels to Sweden to attend a rare midsummer festival at an isolated village. What starts as a beautiful cultural experience quickly turns dark, as they discover they are caught in the rituals of a pagan cult with horrifying intentions.

"Midsommar" is incredibly unsettling, as it subverts typical horror by placing all the horror in daylight and serene settings. It’s visually stunning but profoundly disturbing, pushing boundaries of what’s considered horror by exposing hidden darkness in a seemingly idyllic community. Florence Pugh’s performance is captivating, pulling you into the dread-filled journey.

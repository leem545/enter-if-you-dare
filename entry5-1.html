Cube (1997)


  • Director: Vincenzo Natali
  • Cast: Nicole de Boer, Maurice Dean Wint,
    David Hewlett, Nicky Guadagni, Andrew Miller

A group of strangers finds themselves trapped in a giant, cube-shaped structure filled with deadly traps. They must work together to escape, but the psychological pressure and growing mistrust threaten their survival.

“Cube” was more than just gore; it combined psychological tension with brutal traps. The atmosphere was filled with uncertainty, and seeing how people reacted under extreme pressure was fascinating. I was hooked from the beginning, and the suspense never let up.

Tetsuo: The Iron Man (1989)


  • Director: Shinya Tsukamoto
  • Cast: Tomorowo Taguchi, Kei Fujiwara


After an accident, a man begins to transform, painfully and uncontrollably, into a creature of flesh and metal. His body morphs into a disturbing fusion of human and machine, plunging him into a nightmarish battle for control over his own body.

Known for its surreal, industrial visuals and relentless pacing, Tetsuo pushes body horror to extreme, nightmarish levels. It’s an influential film in the genre, with its unsettling blend of human and machine capturing a unique, disturbing vision of body horror.

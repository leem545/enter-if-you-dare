Monster/Creature Horror

Monster/Creature Horror features monsters or creatures as the main source of fear. These can be mythical beasts, mutants, or unknown beings from nature, creating tension and terror for the characters.

A Quiet Place (2018)


  • Director: John Krasinski
  • Cast: Emily Blunt, John Krasinski,
    Millicent Simmonds, Noah Jupe

In a post-apocalyptic world, a family must live in silence to avoid mysterious creatures that hunt by sound. Any noise could lead to deadly consequences, putting everyone’s lives at risk.

A Quiet Place was one of the most nerve-wracking movie experiences I've ever had. The concept of silence was brilliantly executed, and the tension in each scene was palpable. The creatures were terrifying, but the strength of the family bond was just as moving.
